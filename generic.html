Story

Born in Jacksonville, IL of March 25, 1994. Naveed and his family moved to Dhaka, Bangladesh few after his birth. The Haque's resided there till 2001 living in Olathe, Kansas. Afterwards, they moved to Texarkana, TX where they had lived till 2013. Naveed lives in Dallas, TX with his older brother, who recently just finished medical school, and their mother.

Naveed enjoys making YouTube videos, dancing to various types of music from hip-hop to country, being physically active, camping and enjoying the great outdoors.
